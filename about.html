About this site

This project is an unofficial gallery of wallpapers inspired by Aphex Twin artwork. Images are provided as shared links and intended for personal, non-commercial use only. The site is a minimal static gallery

Credits: images were shared by community contributors. If you own any content here and wish it removed, please contact the site maintainer.
Email: studio.bytelowergames@gmail.com

Want to add a wallpaper? Please send it to the email above.

Plans for the future

  • Make the site dynamic.

    I don't yet have funding for monthly hosting; if you'd like to help with any amount, you can donate at .

  • Allow direct wallpaper uploads.

    Anyone will be able to add wallpapers to the site without emailing — upload and publish directly through the interface.

  • Built-in wallpaper editor.

    A simple online editor (Photopea-like) with ready-made templates and a vector version of the artist's logo to apply to images. It will be possible to export to your computer or send directly to the gallery.

  • Preserve anonymity.

    This site is just a free gallery for fans of the artist's iconography — there will be no account system or forum; anonymity and open access will be a priority.
